Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vulture Bot?

Vulture Bot is a student-built robot that explores how technology can support the role of vultures by detecting and handling animal carcasses more safely.

Why did you choose vultures?

Vultures are important decomposers, but many species are endangered. The project highlights this problem while testing simple robotics and computer vision ideas.

What hardware does the project use?

The prototype is designed around a Raspberry Pi controller, basic sensors, and a small arm or scoop mechanism for interacting with carcass-like targets.
