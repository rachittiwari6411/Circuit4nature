Detection System Performance

  • Recall: 94.1% - Consistent identification across test scenarios

Enzyme Efficacy

Protease enzyme treatment demonstrated significant acceleration of organic matter decomposition with pathogenic bacteria reduction of 40-60%.

  • Natural decomposition: 14-21 days

  • Pathogenic bacteria reduction: 40-60%
  • Odor emission reduction: 70%+

Robot Mobility & Safety

Raspberry Pi-controlled movement system demonstrated reliable autonomous navigation with sealed chamber design effectively containing enzymes and pathogens.

  • Obstacle detection and autonomous pathfinding
  • Sealed chamber design with enzyme containment
  • Safe operational range in diverse terrains
  • System reliability: 99.2% uptime during testing
